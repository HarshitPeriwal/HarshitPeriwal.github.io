About Me

I’m Harshit Periwal, a high school innovator passionate about artificial intelligence, technology, and creativity. I love turning ideas into impact — from developing AI-powered apps to designing educational games that make learning fun.

One of my favorite projects, Eco Bin, is an AI-based waste segregation app that promotes cleaner, smarter communities. I earned a bronze medal (AIR 124) in the Future Tech Olympiad and was Runner-up in the National Game Design Championship for my physics-based game, Garry’s Escape.

Beyond coding, I stay active through tennis, gym workouts, and Model UNs, which keep me balanced and inspired.

At my core, I’m driven by curiosity — to understand how things work and make technology more accessible and impactful for everyone.
