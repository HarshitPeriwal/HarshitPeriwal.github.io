About Me

I’m Harshit Periwal, a high school innovator with a deep passion for artificial intelligence, technology, and creativity. Ever since I first discovered how a few lines of code could bring an idea to life, I’ve been fascinated by the limitless possibilities of tech. Whether it’s developing AI-powered apps or designing educational games that merge fun with learning, I love creating things that have purpose and impact.

One of my proudest projects is Eco Bin, an AI-based waste segregation app that helps promote cleaner, smarter communities through accessible technology. I’ve also earned a bronze medal (AIR 124) in the Future Tech Olympiad and was the Runner-up in the National Game Design Championship, where I created Garry’s Escape — a physics-based game that integrates chemistry and logic into an interactive experience.

Outside of coding, I believe in balance and growth. You’ll often find me at the gym, on the tennis court, participating in Model United Nations, or spending time with friends. These experiences keep me grounded, help me understand different perspectives, and fuel my creativity in unexpected ways.

At my core, I’m driven by curiosity — the desire to understand how things work and how they can work better. My goal is to use technology not just to solve problems, but to make innovation accessible to everyone.
